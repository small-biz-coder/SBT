Our Process

We make it simple to bring your project to life. From initial consultation to final build, we're with you every step of the way — on time, on budget, and with no surprises.

01

Initial Consultation

We start by coming out to your property, walking the space, and listening. We want to understand exactly what you're looking for before we ever talk numbers. No pressure, or sales pitch, we are here to serve you the customer, have a conversation about your project, and what it would take to do the job right.

02

Detailed Estimate

We'll put together a clear, itemized estimate so you know exactly what you're paying for. We offer a wide range of options for materials. If there are alternatives or trade-offs worth considering, we'll walk you through them, choosing the best path for your project. No vague quotes or surprise charges once work begins.

03

Project Scheduling

Once you're ready to move forward, we’ll put you on our schedule. We show up when we say we will — and if anything ever changes, you'll hear from us before you're left wondering. We keep you in the loop throughout the entire project, not just at the beginning and end.

04

Build Your Dream

We get to work and treat your property with respect, that means protecting your existing structures, and your yard. Keeping a clean job-site is something we prioritize. When we're done, we do a final walkthrough with you to make sure everything is right before we call it complete.
